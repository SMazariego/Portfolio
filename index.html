About Me

I have tried on different hats, graduated UCF with an Art degree, worked different jobs. Searched around to find something that stuck. After some time, I settled nicely into carpentry. The environment I found myself in was one where I had a fixed amount of supplies, a chest of tools, and the simple instructions of “This is what it is supposed to look like, this is what it needs to do. Make it happen.” I found an appeal in the freedom of problem solving without a clear direction. I have found a similar satisfaction in coding. There is a goal, both aesthetic and functional that needs to be met and the various coding languages are the tools.
